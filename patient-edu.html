Health Conditions

Stay active: Regular physical activity can improve physical and mental health. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Prevention

Wash your hands: Proper hand hygiene can prevent the spread of germs and infections. Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use hand sanitizer when soap and water are not available.

Lifestyle Changes

Eating a healthy, balanced diet: A diet that is rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats can help patients maintain a healthy weight, reduce their risk of chronic diseases, and improve their energy levels.

Patient Rights

Patients have the right to be fully informed about their medical condition, proposed treatments, and possible risks and benefits, and to give or withhold their consent to treatment.

Support Group

The goal of mental health support groups is to provide a safe and non-judgmental space for individuals to share their experiences, feelings, and concerns related to their mental health conditions. Members of these groups can offer each other practical advice, emotional support, and a sense of community and belonging.
